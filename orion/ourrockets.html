Our Rockets

Model-2A

The Model-2A rocket is the latest addition to our fleet of space vehicles. It is designed to be a reliable and efficient means of transporting payloads and crew into space. With its powerful engines, the Model-2A can reach incredible speeds and altitudes, making it ideal for a variety of missions.

Starting at $95,000 per seat.

The Aurora

Introducing our latest rocket, the Aurora! Designed for speed, precision, and versatility, the Aurora is the pinnacle of modern rocket technology. With a top speed of Mach 8 and a payload capacity of 10 tons, it can transport both crew and cargo to the far reaches of space and beyond.

Starting at $110,000 per seat.

Hypernova X

Designed with cutting-edge technology, the Hypernova X is equipped with a revolutionary propulsion system that uses a combination of liquid and solid rocket fuels to provide maximum thrust and efficiency. Its advanced guidance and navigation system ensures precise trajectory and landing, making it the most reliable rocket in the market.

Starting at $132,000 per seat.

Nova-1

With its sleek design and cutting-edge technology, the Nova-1 is built for speed and efficiency. This rocket is capable of carrying a payload of up to 10,000 pounds into orbit, making it perfect for satellite launches and other space missions. The Nova-1 features advanced propulsion systems, including a high-thrust main engine and four smaller boosters that provide additional thrust during liftoff.

Starting at $160,000 per seat.
